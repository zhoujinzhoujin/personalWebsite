Research

mmprof: Identify Issues Caused by Allocators

(the paper is submitting)

This paper designs the first systematic profiler mmprof to profile the performance, memory overhead, scalability, and application-friendliness of a memory allocator. Based on its report, normal users could adjust the configuration of the allocator or switch to another allocator in order to solve the corresponding issues. Allocator designers could identify design and implementation issues, without the need of implementing a profiler for every allocator. Based on our extensive evaluation, mmprof helps report performance and memory issues caused by an allocator, and help identify multiple known and unknown design issues in widely-used allocators.

Open Source: https://github.com/UTSASRG/mmprof
